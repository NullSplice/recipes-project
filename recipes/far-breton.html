Far Breton

Description

Far Breton is a traditional Brittany baked custard—firm like a flan—often studded with prunes, lightly sweet and eggy.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 120 g flour
  • 80 g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch salt
  • 100-150 g pitted prunes
  • Butter for the dish

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 300°C. Butter a 22cm round dish.
  2. Whisk eggs + sugar + vanilla. Add flour, then milk -> smooth batter.
  3. Scatter prunes in dish, pour batter, dot with a little butter.
  4. Bake 35-40 min until puffed and golden. Rest 10 min. Eat warm or cold.

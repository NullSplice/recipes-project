Teriyaki Chichen Bowl

Description

Sweet-savory chicken with rice and veggies.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • rice
  • broccoli

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, sugar, ginger, garlic = sauce.
  2. Pan-fry chicken, add sauce, simmer.
  3. Steam broccoli.
  4. Serve chicken and broccoli over rice.

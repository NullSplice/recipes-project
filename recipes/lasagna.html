Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta of wide sheets layered with ragù or vegetables, béchamel or ricotta, and cheese, baked until bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filing
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Etc.

Steps

  1. Boli pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with oinon and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bwol.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.

Crêpe

Description

A crêpe is a thin, delicate French pancake, quickly cooked and folded or rolled around sweet or savory fillings.

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 500 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp metled butter
  • Pinch salt

Steps

  1. Whisk everything until smooth
  2. Rest 10 min
  3. Hot lightly-buttered pan. Pour a thin layer, swirl.
  4. Cook ~1 min, 20-30 sec. Repeat
